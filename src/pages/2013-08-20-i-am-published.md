<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
  Well, sorta. Team-effort published.&nbsp;</p> 
  
  <div>
  </div>
  
  <div>
    I guess you could say that I&#8217;m a published illustrator. Never in five billion years did I think I&#8217;d illustrate a book before writing one &#8212; I didn&#8217;t even know Photoshop until this year. Ha. Life does funny things to you like that.&nbsp;
  </div>
  
  <div>
  </div>
  
  <div>
    But yeah! After a summer interning with the English Department&#8217;s <i>Voices: On Stage and In Print </i>program, we churned out a pretty amazing book. I only take credit for the cover and a bit of editing. The inside is just as magnificent, if not more so. &nbsp;
  </div>
  
  <div>
  </div>
  
  <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
  </div>
  
  <p>
    &nbsp;What an amazing experience!
